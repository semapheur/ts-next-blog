# Lorentzian manifolds

<MathBox title='Lorentzian manifold' boxType='definition'>
A *Lorentzian manifold* is a pseudo-Riemannian manifold $M$ equipped with a metric $g$ with signature $(n-1, 1)$ or $(1, n-1)$, where $n$ is the dimension of $M$.
</MathBox>
